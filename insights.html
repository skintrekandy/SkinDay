Trends & Risks

Emerging market signals

Four structural shifts are reshaping the Canadian aesthetic market. Each carries implications for brand positioning, channel strategy, and pricing integrity.

Price Compression
32% of clinics are pricing below $7/unit
The budget segment is growing, particularly in suburban Ontario and new-entrant markets. Aggressive discounting puts pressure on mid-market pricing integrity across all brands.
Rating Inflation
61% of clinics are rated 4.9–5.0 on Google
Selective review solicitation has compressed ratings into an artificially narrow band. Ratings no longer differentiate clinics — credential and pricing transparency are becoming the real competitive signals.
Market Expansion
Rapid clinic density growth outside the GTA
Secondary markets — Halifax, Winnipeg, Saskatoon — are emerging as meaningful clinic populations. National distribution coverage built around Ontario alone will increasingly miss volume.
Channel Shift
Non-physician injectors now dominate by volume
RN and NP-led clinics represent 73% of the credentialed market. Brand education and rep coverage strategies designed around physician channels are reaching a shrinking share of active injectors.
