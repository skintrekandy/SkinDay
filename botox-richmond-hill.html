Common Questions

Botox in Richmond Hill — FAQ

How much does Botox cost in Richmond Hill?
Botox in Richmond Hill typically ranges from $9 to $14 per unit, depending on the injector's credentials and the clinic's positioning. MD-injected clinics tend to charge $11–$14/unit, while RN-injected clinics are often $9–$11/unit. A full forehead treatment (20–30 units) runs approximately $180–$420. SkinDay shows you the verified per-unit price before you call.
Which areas of Richmond Hill have the most cosmetic clinics?
The highest concentration of medical aesthetics clinics in Richmond Hill is along the Yonge Street corridor — particularly around Yonge & Sheppard, Yonge & Finch, and the Richmond Hill Centre area. There is also a growing cluster near Bathurst & Wilson and along Leslie Street.
What credentials should my Botox injector have in Richmond Hill?
In Ontario, Botox (and other neurotoxins) must be prescribed and injected by a regulated healthcare professional — typically a physician (MD), nurse practitioner (NP), or registered nurse (RN) working under a physician's delegation. SkinDay displays injector credential badges (MD, NP, RN/RPN) on every clinic card so you can filter by what matters to you.
Is it safe to choose a clinic based on the lowest price?
Price is one factor, but it doesn't tell the whole story. SkinDay shows price alongside injector credentials and Google reviews so you can make an informed choice. A lower price from an experienced MD may be better value than a higher price from a less experienced injector — or vice versa. The goal is fit, not just cost.
